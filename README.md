I started out in full-stack development and now spend most of my time building Solar1, from product and software to customer research and go-to-market.

Solar1 is being built to help solar installers manage their business in one place instead of relying on spreadsheets, messages, and disconnected tools. The goal is to connect the full journey from lead to PTO across sales, projects, permitting, field operations, inventory, finance, and the teams behind them.

### What I’m working on

- Building Solar1 from the ground up
- Speaking with solar teams and learning how their operations actually work
- Turning real operational problems into practical product workflows
- Working across product, engineering, design, marketing, and distribution

### Background

Before Solar1, I worked on full-stack web products and explored projects across SaaS, marketplaces, internal tools, and business software.
